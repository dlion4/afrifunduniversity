Benefits of Student Loan Refinancing

Lower Your Monthly Payment

When you refinance your student loans with College Ave, you can choose a brand new loan term between 5 and 20 years. A longer loan term can help to lower your monthly payments, but it may increase the total amount of interest you'll pay on the loan overall. This can give you a little extra money each month to put toward other expenses.

Simplify Your Payments

If you have multiple loans from college, they are likely on different repayment schedules. Keeping track of multiple different loans, payment schedules, interest accruals, and due dates can be daunting. Refinancing your student loans allows you to combine all your loans into one new loan.

Save Money

Depending on what loans you have, when you took them out, and what your interest rates are, you may have a high interest rate. If you have good credit and a stable income, you may qualify for a lower interest rate. A lower interest rate can help you save hundreds or even thousands of dollars over the duration of your loan repayment.

Pay Off Your Loans Faster

Refinancing your student loans gives you the option to choose a shorter loan term. This change can help you pay off your student loans earlier than your original repayment plan. Chipping away at your loans faster will allow you to be free from student loan debt faster.

Flexible Term Options

We’ll help you choose how many years you take to pay back your refi loan so you can be confident that your loan is working for you. We offer 5 year to 10 year repayment term options3.
