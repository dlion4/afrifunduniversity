Did you know?

You can use your student loan to cover costs beyond tuition and room & board. This includes books, transportation, and other supplies which can really add up. For reference, here are typical other expenses from the Afri Fund's Average Estimated Undergraduate Budgets, 2024-25:
Although to apply for more of this you'll need to have a collateral.

  • Transportation - KES 100,000
  • Laptop & Other Supplies - KES 200,000
