About Us

Afri Fund started with a big vision and a simple mission - help students prepare for a bright future.

We love providing an altogether different (and uncomplicated!) experience. We're former students, and many of us are parents, so we get where the stress of preparing for, paying for and going to college comes from. Our personal and professional experiences have given us the skills to send stress packing. We're committed to delivering practical and personal solutions that give young adults a running start on their roads to financial success. We don't just like to say it, we built our business around it: when it comes to college, "You've got this, we've got you."

Our Core Values

Simple

Seriously. Smooth and straightforward is what we're all about. Whether you’re on a mobile device or your desktop, we make it easy.

Personal
